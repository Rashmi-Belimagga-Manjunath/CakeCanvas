About

Handcrafted with care, designed for your moment

CakeCanvas is a London-based online bakery specialising in fully bespoke celebration cakes. Every creation is designed and baked from scratch by our team of skilled artisans, using the finest ingredients — real butter, Belgian chocolate, and Madagascar vanilla.

From whimsical birthday cakes to elegant wedding centrepieces and corporate showstoppers, we treat every order as a unique work of art.

✦ Bespoke designs for any occasion
✦ Eggless, gluten-free, vegan & nut-free options
✦ Free delivery within 25 miles of London
✦ AI assistant for instant guidance — no waiting
How It Works

From inspiration to celebration

Step 1

Tell us your vision

Chat with our AI assistant about your occasion, share an inspiration photo, and answer a few quick questions.

Step 2

Review your brief

We compile your preferences, photo, and requirements into a structured brief for our design team.

Step 3

Designer creates

One of our skilled bakers reviews your brief and crafts a personalised cake with premium ingredients.

Step 4

Delivery & celebration

White-glove delivery within 25 miles of London, or collect from our bakery. Your celebration begins.
