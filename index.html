About

Handcrafted with love,
designed for your moment

CakeCanvas is an online bakery based in London, specialising in fully customised celebration cakes. Every cake is designed and baked from scratch by our team of skilled designers and bakers — using premium ingredients like real butter, Belgian chocolate, and Madagascar vanilla.

Whether it's a whimsical birthday cake, an elegant wedding centrepiece, or a corporate showstopper, we treat every order as a unique work of art.

  • Custom designs for any occasion
  • Eggless, gluten-free, vegan & nut-free options
  • Free 25-mile delivery from our London bakery
  • AI chatbot for instant guidance — no waiting
How it works

From inspiration to celebration

💬

Chat with our AI assistant

Tell us your occasion, share an inspiration photo, and answer a few quick questions. Our chatbot guides you step by step — no forms, no waiting.

📸

Instant photo feedback

Upload a photo and our tool checks the quality instantly — blur, lighting, resolution. If it's not quite right, we'll tell you exactly how to improve it.

👩‍🍳

Designer reviews your brief

Your requirements, photo, and preferences are compiled into a structured brief. A designer reviews it within 24 hours and sends you a personalised quote.

🎉

Delivery or collection

We deliver within 25 miles of London, with white-glove setup included. Prefer to collect? Book an appointment at our bakery studio.
