About

Where tradition meets imagination

🍰 ✨ 🌸

CakeCanvas is an online bakery based in London, specialising in fully customised celebration cakes. Every cake is designed and baked from scratch by our team of skilled artisan designers — using premium ingredients like real butter, Belgian chocolate, and Madagascar vanilla.

Whether it's a whimsical birthday cake, an elegant wedding centrepiece, or a corporate showstopper, we treat every order as a unique work of art.

  • ✦ Bespoke designs for any occasion
  • ✦ Eggless, gluten-free, vegan & nut-free options
  • ✦ Complimentary 25-mile delivery from London
  • ✦ AI chatbot for instant guidance — no waiting
